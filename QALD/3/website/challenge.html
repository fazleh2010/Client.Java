QALD-3 offers two open challenges:

  • Task 1: Multilingual question answering
  • Task 2: Ontology lexicalization

In September 2013, there will be a half-day evaluation lab at CLEF 2013 comprising the presentation of results and short demos of the participating systems, as well as a general discussion about possible further directions of research and future challenges. The program will be made available here in August 2013.
