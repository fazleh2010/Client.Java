Open Challenge

The workshop is preceded by an open challenge in Question Answering over Linked Data, in order to provide a benchmark for comparing different approaches and systems that mediate between a user (expressing his or her information need in natural language) and semantic data.

Full description: qald-2-challenge.pdf (last updated: March 26, 2012)
